Recommendation

"Understand front-end frameworks: Familiarize yourself with popular front-end frameworks like React, Angular, or Vue.js. These frameworks help you build complex and scalable front-end applications."

"Gain proficiency in back-end development: Learn a server-side programming language such as JavaScript (Node.js), Python (Django or Flask), Ruby (Ruby on Rails), or PHP (Laravel)."

"Build projects and contribute to open-source: Practical experience is invaluable. Work on personal projects to apply your knowledge and develop a portfolio."

"Continuously learn and stay updated: The field of web development is constantly evolving. Stay curious, follow industry trends, explore new technologies, and keep enhancing your skills to adapt to changing demands."
